WHAT WE DO

A Trusted Platform for
Influence and Impact

Simphiwe Mtetwa is a trusted platform for influence and impact, strategically connecting South Africa's foremost decision-makers with the changemakers driving meaningful progress.

As Africa's leading source for Corporate Social Investment (CSI) intelligence, we deliver well-researched, forward-thinking content; produce professionally designed CSI reports for corporates, NGOs and foundations; convene high-level, results-driven events; and encourage fresh perspectives that challenge conventional thinking.

Our work is founded on a rigorous understanding of South Africa's CSI dynamics, a charged political landscape and our clients' unique needs. For this reason, we invest strongly in knowledge development, learning and capability building.

Our insightful thought leadership pieces on Africa's CSI markets, trends and emerging best practices have proved invaluable to CSI leaders and practitioners across the spectrum – whether corporate managers or NGO activists.
